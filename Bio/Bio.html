Arielle Tolbert is an ambitious, hardworking, and talented young woman from Southfield, Michigan that loves to give her all and be involved in the community. She has the knowledge, perseverance, and passion to reach the next level and will do whatever it takes to achieve all of my dreams.

She is a current student at Michigan State University, where she studies Computer Engineering. She has always had a passion for computers, but her love for technology incomparable to her love for acting and modeling. Arielle Tolbert has been modeling and acting for years, but did not get serious about this passion until she was 16 and decided to enroll in Michigans Academy of Creative Artistic Productions modeling & acting class. From there her love for the industry grew even more as became more confortable in front of the camera and on the runway and gained professional experience. Since then, Arielle is actively involved in Michigan States theatre program and is constantly seeking to enhance her talents and desires.

Recently, Arielle Tolbert has sought to use her talents and skills to create and co-host a new Michigan State University web show called Fire & Ice Conversations. On there she works side-by-side with her co-host to plan, promote, host, and edit each episode. The purpose of the web show aims to focus on tackling conflicting topics within the community and bring awareness to issues that are often overlooked.

Arielles ambitions do not stop there. She is also very involved in heath and fitness. Not only is she passionate about maintaining a healthy lifestyle for herself she loves to inspire others to lead a healthy lifestyle as well. She is a certified personal trainer and group fitness leader and the author of the health and fitness book, How to Gain the Freshman 15 Of Muscle.

As a firm believer in good health and public service, Arielle Tolbert is also very actively involved the Detroit YMCA. From the age of 14, Arielle has been positively impacted by the many programs that the YMCA has to offer and feels that is only right to give back to those who so effortlessly gave to her. The YMCA has become Arielles second home because of how close she has become to those within that community and the inspiration so many there have had on her life. Arielle aims to impact other young adults the way that so many have impacted her within her involvement at the YMCA and in the YMCAs I Can Achieve Program by constantly providing a helping hand to the organization, inspiring the youth to believe in themselves and work hard for their dreams, and serving as a role model to young women.
